Website Design Brief – Homepage Hero Slider (TS Website)
Objective

Create a visually engaging homepage hero slider that immediately captures attention, communicates the brand message, and encourages visitors to explore the website. Every slide should be bold, memorable, and seamlessly connected to the overall visual identity of the site.

Hero Slider Direction
Replicate the successful hero slider concept used on the existing TS website.
Use a sequence of engaging images, graphics, short video clips, or montage-style visuals that create movement and tell a story.
The slider should feel modern, dynamic, and immersive rather than static.
Each slide should contain a short, impactful message that enhances the visitor's experience.
Key Messaging

One slide should be dedicated exclusively to the brand slogan:

Discover More, Spend Less

This slogan should be treated as a feature slide, not simply another message.

Slogan Slide Requirements
The slogan must be the primary focal point.
It should stand out more prominently than every other slide.
Use:
Deep maroon (Queensland-inspired) background.
Large white typography.
Minimal distractions.
The design should be bold, clean, and memorable.
Avoid overcrowding the slide with additional elements.
Visual Style
Use deep, rich background colours that create strong contrast.
White typography should remain highly legible.
Slides should blend together cohesively while each maintains its own identity.
Consider creative layouts rather than standard full-screen images.
Where appropriate, combine three or four complementary images into a single creative collage or montage instead of using conventional single-image slides.
Creative Direction
Incorporate montage techniques to communicate ideas quickly through a combination of:
Images
Video snippets
Graphics
Layered visual storytelling
Transitions should feel smooth and premium.
Designer's Creative Freedom

Prabh is encouraged to:

Select the strongest visuals from the existing website's media library.
Reuse existing content creatively.
Apply professional design judgement to determine which images and layouts create the greatest visual impact.
Ensure the featured slogan slide integrates naturally with the overall sequence while remaining the most visually dominant.
Design Priority

The overriding design principle is:

Every slide should stand out, but the "Discover More, Spend Less" slide must stand out the most.

The homepage hero section should leave visitors with a strong first impression, balancing creativity, clarity, and brand consistency.
